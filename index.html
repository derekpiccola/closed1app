Privacy Policy

Email Privacy Policy

The only personal information we collect is your email address. We only use your email address on an "opt-in" basis to notify you when the Closed1 app launches. We will not send you spam. We do not disclose your email address to 3rd parties.

We will never share, sell, or rent individual personal information with anyone without your advance permission or unless ordered by a court of law. Information submitted to us is only available to employees who manage this information for purposes of contacting you or sending you emails based on your request for information and to contracted service providers for purposes of providing services relating to our communications with you.

Each email sent contains an easy, automated way for you to cease receiving emails from us, or to change your expressed interests. If you wish to do this, simply follow the instructions at the end of any email. If you have received unwanted, unsolicited emails sent via this system or purporting to be sent via this system, please forward a copy of that email with your comments to info@closedoneapp.com for removal from our mailing list.
